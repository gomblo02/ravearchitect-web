Modern Tropical House

Set in the lush city of Malang, this two-story residence merges clean modern lines with tropical warmth. The use of natural textures and dynamic shading elements brings a sense of balance between indoor comfort and outdoor connection — a bold yet breathable design that embraces the spirit of its environment.

Description

Nestled in the heart of Malang, this modern tropical residence was crafted for W.H., a client seeking harmony between sophistication and simplicity. Designed across two floors, the house embraces a warm, contemporary aesthetic while maximizing natural light and airflow — a hallmark of tropical design.

At the entrance, a lush front garden and shaded carport create a welcoming threshold. Inside, the ground floor opens into a spacious living room that blends seamlessly with the dining area and kitchen, offering a clean, open-concept layout ideal for both intimate moments and entertaining guests.

The home features two bedrooms and two bathrooms, each carefully considered for both privacy and comfort. Throughout the interior, natural materials and earthy tones balance modern finishes — creating a sense of calm, grounded elegance.

This is not just a residence — it's a retreat. A modern tropical expression of everyday life made serene.
