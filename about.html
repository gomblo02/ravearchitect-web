About Us

Founded in 2021 by four architecture graduates, Ravearchitect is a design studio based in Sidoarjo, Indonesia. We specialize in architecture, interior, and landscape design — creating spaces that are personal, purposeful, and built to last.

We Design With Meaning

At Ravearchitect, we believe that every space should carry a story — not just of bricks and concrete, but of emotions, routines, memories, and dreams. Architecture, interior, and landscape are not separate fields to us — they are parts of a single, living experience. A good design speaks gently: it embraces light, breathes with its surroundings, and grows with the people within it. We don’t design for trends — we design for time. And through every project, big or small, we aim to shape spaces that make people feel truly at home — seen, supported, and inspired.

About

Ravearchitect was founded in May 2021 by four architecture graduates driven by the same vision — to shape meaningful spaces that connect people and place. Based in Sedatigede, Sidoarjo, our studio has completed numerous projects across architecture, interior, and landscape design. We believe that great design is not just about beauty, but also about creating environments that feel personal, timeless, and inspiring. At Ravearchitect, we continue to grow, explore, and design with purpose. As we move forward, we remain committed to crafting designs that leave a lasting impression — on people, places, and the planet.
